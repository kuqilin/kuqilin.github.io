三、LaTeX 语法全测试

3.1 行内公式（基础）

$a + b = c$ $\sqrt{2} \approx 1.414$ $\sin(\pi/2) = 1$ $x^2 + y^2 = r^2$ $\frac{1}{2} + \frac{1}{3} = \frac{5}{6}$

3.2 块级公式（基础）

$$ a^2 + 2ab + b^2 = (a + b)^2 $$ $$ \lim_{x \to \infty} \frac{1}{x} = 0 $$ $$ \int_{0}^{1} x^2 dx = \frac{1}{3} $$

3.3 代数/数论公式

$$ \forall x \in \mathbb{R}, \exists y \in \mathbb{N}, \text{s.t. } y > x $$ $$ a_n = a_1 + (n-1)d \quad (\text{等差数列通项}) $$ $$ S_n = \frac{a_1(1-q^n)}{1-q} \quad (q \neq 1, \text{等比数列求和}) $$ $$ \gcd(a,b) \times \text{lcm}(a,b) = |a \times b| $$

3.4 微积分公式

$$ \frac{dy}{dx} = \lim_{\Delta x \to 0} \frac{f(x+\Delta x) - f(x)}{\Delta x} $$ $$ \int_{a}^{b} f’(x) dx = f(b) - f(a) \quad (\text{牛顿-莱布尼茨公式}) $$ $$ \iint_{D} f(x,y) dxdy \quad (\text{二重积分}) $$ $$ \oint_{C} Pdx + Qdy = \iint_{D} \left( \frac{\partial Q}{\partial x} - \frac{\partial P}{\partial y} \right) dxdy \quad (\text{格林公式}) $$

3.5 线性代数公式

$$ \mathbf{A} = \begin{pmatrix} a_{11} & a_{12} & \dots & a_{1n} \ a_{21} & a_{22} & \dots & a_{2n} \ \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \ a_{m1} & a_{m2} & \dots & a_{mn} \end{pmatrix} $$ $$ \det(\mathbf{A}) = \begin{vmatrix} 1 & 2 \ 3 & 4 \end{vmatrix} = 1 \times 4 - 2 \times 3 = -2 $$ $$ \mathbf{A} \mathbf{x} = \lambda \mathbf{x} \quad (\text{特征值方程}) $$ $$ \text{rank}(\mathbf{A}) = \text{rank}(\mathbf{A}^T) $$

3.6 集合论公式

$$ A \cup B = {x \mid x \in A \lor x \in B} $$ $$ A \cap B = {x \mid x \in A \land x \in B} $$ $$ A \setminus B = {x \mid x \in A \land x \notin B} $$ $$ \emptyset \subseteq A \subseteq U \quad (U \text{为全集}) $$ $$ \bigcup_{i=1}^{n} A_i, \bigcap_{i=1}^{n} A_i $$

3.7 逻辑/命题公式

$$ P \implies Q \quad (\text{蕴含}) $$ $$ P \iff Q \quad (\text{等价}) $$ $$ \neg P \quad (\text{非}) $$ $$ P \lor Q \quad (\text{或}), \quad P \land Q \quad (\text{与}) $$ $$ \exists x \in A, P(x) \quad (\text{存在量词}), \quad \forall x \in A, P(x) \quad (\text{全称量词}) $$

3.8 几何公式

$$ V_{\text{球}} = \frac{4}{3} \pi R^3 \quad (\text{球的体积}) $$ $$ S_{\text{圆台}} = \pi (r_1 + r_2) l + \pi r_1^2 + \pi r_2^2 \quad (\text{圆台表面积}) $$ $$ \cos C = \frac{a^2 + b^2 - c^2}{2ab} \quad (\text{余弦定理}) $$ $$ \frac{a}{\sin A} = \frac{b}{\sin B} = \frac{c}{\sin C} = 2R \quad (\text{正弦定理}) $$

3.9 特殊函数/符号

$$ \ln x, \log_a x, e^x, \exp(x) $$ $$ \alpha, \beta, \gamma, \delta, \epsilon, \pi, \theta, \lambda, \mu, \sigma, \omega $$ $$ \geq, \leq, \neq, \approx, \equiv, \propto, \in, \notin, \subseteq, \supseteq $$ $$ \sum, \prod, \lim, \int, \oint, \nabla, \Delta, \partial $$ $$ \vec{a}, \overrightarrow{AB}, \bar{x}, \hat{\theta} $$

3.10 多行公式（对齐/分段）

公式对齐（align）

$$ \begin{align*} f(x) &= x^2 + 2x + 1 \ &= (x+1)^2 \ g(x) &= \sin x + \cos x \ &= \sqrt{2} \sin\left(x + \frac{\pi}{4}\right) \end{align*} $$

分段函数（cases）

$$ f(x) = \begin{cases} x, & x > 0 \ 0, & x = 0 \ -x, & x < 0 \end{cases} $$

3.11 公式标注
